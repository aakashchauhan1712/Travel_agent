TRAVEL GUIDELINES

Bali, Indonesia:
- Indian passport holders can enter Indonesia under the current visa policies. Travelers should always verify the latest visa requirements before departure.
- Passport validity should generally be at least 6 months from the date of entry.
- Travel insurance is recommended.

Thailand:
- Passport validity should generally be at least 6 months.
- Travelers should verify current visa and entry requirements before travel.
- Travel insurance is recommended.

Singapore:
- Travelers should verify visa requirements before departure.
- Passport validity should generally be at least 6 months.
- Carry proof of accommodation and return travel if required.

General International Travel:
- Keep digital and physical copies of your passport.
- Inform your bank before international travel.
- Purchase travel insurance when possible.
- Check currency exchange rates before departure.
- Verify local emergency contact numbers.

General Domestic Travel:
- Carry a valid government ID.
- Keep emergency contacts accessible.
- Monitor local weather conditions.
